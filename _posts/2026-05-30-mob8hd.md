## components

- happymodel mobula8 frame
- happymodel ex1103-kv11000 motors 
- gemfan 2023-3
- JHEMCU GHF435AIO (ArteryTek AT32F435 288MHz MCU, 20A Bluejay DSHOT600 ESCs)
- LHCP antenna
- Walksnail 1S light V3
- canopy https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:6059209
- 550mah 

## weight

- dry: 57.7

## prop tests

- 2023-3 clear (pitch 2.3", 52mm) with M2x6 screws: hover throttle 34, hover 18k-21k rpm, high gyro noise
- 2023-3 green (pitch 2.3", 52mm): hover throttle 33, hover 18k-21k rpm, low gyro noise
- D2-3 (pitch 2", 50.8mm) with M2x6 screws: hover throttle 35, hover 18k-20k rpm, low gyro noise
- 2014-3 (pitch 1.4", 50.79mm): hover throttle 41, hover 22k-27k rpm, low gyro noise
